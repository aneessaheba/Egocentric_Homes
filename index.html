What is Ego Homes?

Ego Homes is a first-person dataset of everyday household activities captured in native home environments. Using head-mounted and body-worn cameras, we record unscripted interactions across the entire home like kitchen, laundry, living spaces. Annotated with hand pose, segmentation, and action labels.
